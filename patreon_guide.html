How to Connect Patreon to Discord & Join The Militia Trading Company Server

Back to Home

Scenario 1: Connecting Patreon to Discord (If Not Connected Yet)

Step 1: Log in to Patreon

  1. Go to Patreon.com and log into your account.
  2. Click your profile icon in the top-left corner.
  3. Select Settings from the dropdown menu.

Step 2: Connect Discord

  1. In Settings, click More > Connected Apps.
  2. Find Discord and click Connect.
  3. Log in to your Discord account and click Authorize to sync Patreon with Discord.

Step 3: Join The Militia Trading Company Server

  1. After connecting, Patreon should automatically add you to our Discord server.
  2. If not, go to Patreon > Memberships > The Militia Trading Company and click Join Discord.

Scenario 2: Fixing the Wrong Discord Account Connection

Step 1: Unlink Discord from Patreon

  1. Go to Patreon.com and log in.
  2. Click your profile icon and select Settings.
  3. Click More > Connected Apps, find Discord, and click Disconnect.

Step 2 (Optional): Remove Patreon from Your Discord Connections

  1. Open Discord and go to User Settings ⚙️.
  2. Navigate to the Connections tab.
  3. Find Patreon and click the X to remove it.

Step 3: Reconnect Patreon to the Correct Discord Account

  1. Log out of Discord completely.
  2. Go back to Patreon and refresh the page.
  3. Reconnect Patreon to Discord by following Scenario 1: Step 2.

Scenario 3: Joining The Militia Trading Company Server After Connecting Patreon

Step 1: Confirm Your Patreon & Discord Are Linked

  1. Go to Patreon > Settings > More > Connected Apps.
  2. Ensure that Discord is connected.

Step 2: Join The Militia Trading Company Server

  1. Go to Patreon.com and click your profile icon.
  2. Select Memberships from the dropdown menu.
  3. Find The Militia Trading Company and click Join Server.

Step 3: Manually Join Using a Direct Link (If Needed)

  1. Ensure you're logged into the correct Discord account.
  2. Click this invite link: [Your Server Invite Link]
  3. Once in the server, type !verify in the verification channel if required.

Step 4: Check Your Role & Access

  1. Wait a few minutes for Patreon’s bot to sync your role.
  2. If you don’t have access, try leaving and rejoining the server.

Still Having Issues?

If you’ve tried everything and still have trouble:

  • Refresh Patreon & Discord
  • Log out and log back in
  • Contact a moderator in Discord
  • Contact Patreon Support
