🚀 1. Respect the Mission

We are here to focus, master, and build independence. Please avoid distractions, hype, and jumping between strategies. This server is grounded in our core principles:

  • One focused trading method
  • Mastery over variety
  • Zero tolerance for gimmicks, fads, or fearmongering

🧠 2. Speak with Respect

Treat others how you want to be treated—like serious, focused people working toward freedom. Not allowed:

  • Harassment, bullying, hate speech
  • Personal attacks, sarcasm meant to belittle
  • Arguments with moderators or staff (see section 6)

📵 3. No Spam, Scams, or Self-Promotion

This includes:

  • Promoting your own group, link, or service without approval
  • DMing members unsolicited offers or advice
  • Pump-and-dump schemes or shady affiliate links

Violators will be banned—no questions asked.

💬 4. Use Channels as Intended

Help keep the server organized:

  • #trading-talk – for technical and market discussions
  • #general – for casual chat or quick questions
  • #support – for help with access, billing, or platform issues

If you're not sure where to post, ask a mod before you clutter up the server.

⚠️ 5. Stay Within the Law

This community is built on trust and transparency. That means:

  • No illegal activity of any kind
  • No sharing of copyrighted content
  • No impersonating other members or staff

We report serious violations to Discord and, if needed, legal authorities.

🛡️ 6. Moderator Authority

Mods are here to keep the space focused and safe. Their word is final in the moment. If you disagree with a moderation decision:

  • Do not argue in public threads or chats
  • Instead, DM a staff member or use the support channel

Repeat offenders or drama creators will be removed.

🔒 7. Keep It Private

Everything shared in this server should stay in the server. Do not:

  • Screenshot and share private convos
  • Copy, distribute, or repurpose educational materials without permission
  • Leak server content externally—we take confidentiality seriously

📚 8. Learn First, Then Ask

We encourage questions—but read pinned messages, guides, and past discussions first. This is a space for engaged learners, not passive consumers.

✅ 9. Agree & Participate

By participating in this server, you agree to:

  • Follow these guidelines
  • Respect our Terms of Service
  • Help us create a space where mastery and growth thrive

We reserve the right to update these guidelines as needed. Thanks for helping us build something real.
